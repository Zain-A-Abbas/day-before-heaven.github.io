DAY BEFORE HEAVEN

ABOUT

Day Before Heaven is a roguelike monster-catcher focused on building a powerful team of mythological daemons, collecting chaotic artifacts, and fighting challenging bosses. The game is currently in an open beta phase, with testing focusing on improving gameplay balance and bugfixing at the moment.

The apocalypse has passed, and you play as one of the few remaining humans left, with a singular goal: To sit upon the throne of Paradise, and remake the world in your image. Along this path, you will strike down countless daemons and take their power for your own ends.

FEATURES

  • Randomized level layout, rewards, and bosses per playthrough!
  • Difficult, challenging boss fights that demand preparation and careful planning!
  • Hit weaknesses and critical hits to maximize your turns, while deploying daemons to nullify and repel enemy attacks to erase their own turns!
  • 40+ daemons to battle, recruit, and fuse together!
  • Over 90 chaotic artifacts to obtain and equip on characters!
  • Experience the power progression of a full turn-based RPG in just one hour!
